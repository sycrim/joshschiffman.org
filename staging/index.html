Welcome

Dr. Joshua Schiffman is a Distinguished Technologist and Research Director in HP Inc.’s Security Lab, where he leads the company’s research agenda in device lifecycle security management and supply chain security. Dr. Schiffman chairs the Trusted Computing Group’s Technical Committee and Supply Chain Security Working Group. He received his PhD in computer science and engineering at Pennsylvania State University and has over 27 peer-reviewed publications and 30 patents in computer security technology.
